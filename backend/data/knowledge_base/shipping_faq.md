# Shipping FAQ

## Where is my order?
Track your order using the link in your shipping confirmation email, or go to Account > Orders > Track. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, express shipping takes 2-3 business days.

## My package appears to be lost
If tracking shows no movement for 7 or more days, email shipping@company.com with your order ID. We will open an investigation with the carrier and reship your order if it is confirmed lost.

## I received the wrong item
Photograph the wrong item and the packaging label. Email support@company.com within 14 days of delivery. We will send the correct item with a prepaid return label at no cost to you.

## What are international shipping times?
Canada and Mexico: 7-10 business days. Europe: 10-14 business days. Asia and Australia: 14-21 business days. Customs processing may add 3-7 additional days.

## Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
Shipping address changes are only possible before the order ships, usually within 1 hour of placing the order. Contact support immediately if you need to change the address.

## My package says delivered but I did not receive it
Check with neighbors and around your property first. Then wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages delivered early. If still missing after 24 hours, email shipping@company.com with your order ID and we will file a carrier claim.
