# Account FAQ

## How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days per our privacy policy.

## How do I change my email address?
Go to Settings > Profile > Email > Edit. A verification link is sent to your new email address. Your old email remains active until you complete verification.

## How do I set up two-factor authentication?
Go to Settings > Security > Enable 2FA. Use an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy. Make sure to save your backup codes in a safe place.

## I am locked out of my account
After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes automatically. For immediate unlock, you can verify your identity with our support team using the email address on your account.

## How do I change my username?
Username changes are limited to once every 30 days. Go to Settings > Profile > Username to make a change.

## How do I transfer my account to a new email?
Contact support@company.com from your current registered email. We will verify your identity and help you transfer the account. This process takes 1-2 business days.
