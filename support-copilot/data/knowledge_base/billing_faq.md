# Billing FAQ

## How do I update my payment method?
Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. Click "Add new card" and enter your details. Old cards can be removed by clicking the trash icon next to them.

## Why was I charged twice?
Duplicate charges usually settle within 3-5 business days as a pending authorization. If both charges are still posted after 7 days, contact billing@company.com with both transaction IDs and we will investigate immediately.

## How do I get a refund?
Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase. Email billing@company.com with your order ID. Refunds process in 5-10 business days back to the original payment method.

## How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. No partial refunds are available for monthly plans.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Bank transfers are available for Enterprise plans only.

## I was charged after cancelling
If you were charged after cancellation, email billing@company.com immediately with your cancellation confirmation date and the charge date. We will issue a full refund if the cancellation was confirmed before the billing date.
